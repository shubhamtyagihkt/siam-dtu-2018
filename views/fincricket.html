Description

"Nobody has ever bet enough on a winning horse.” Ever dreamt of owning an IPL franchise, built and bought by YOU? Do you have what it takes to cut through palpable stress and become a part of the summit's most grand-scale event? Then,stay right here,because IPL Auction is the place for you! Indian Premier League has become a huge annual affair. But,What happens before IPL? An auction, that allocates team to players, as chosen by the owners of the teams. So, here's a chance for all the cricket fanatics, to make their very own dream team and discover the unique parrallelism between cricket and finance. Make the right investment, use the allocated funds judiciously and you will end up with the right fit for your team. Bid wisely, DON'T GET BOLD.

Rules

  1. 10 teams advance to the bidding contest on the basis of a cricket quiz consisting of 15 questions based on cricket trivia and basic intelligence.
  2. Each team has to make a squad of 7 players consisting of 2 batsmen, 2 bowlers, 2 all-rounders and 1 wicketkeeper.
  3. The budget given to each team is 3,00,000 INR.
  4. There are 112 players which includes 32 batsmen, 32 bowlers, 32 all-rounders and 16 wicketkeepers.
  5. List of players with their base price and value points shall be given to each team beforehand.
  6. The team with maximum value points would be the winner. In case of a tie, the team with maximum amount of budget left would be the winner.
  7. There is only 1 round of bidding i.e players once unsold would not return afterwards.
  8. The process of bidding is secretive i.e first price sealed bid auction. For each player, each team will be required to submit a bid containing the maximum amount they can spend on that player. The bid would not be known to the other teams. The player would subsequently be sold to the team with the highest individual bid. If the highest individual bid is a tie, player would be sold to the second highest individual bid.
