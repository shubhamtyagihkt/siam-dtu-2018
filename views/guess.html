Description

It is an event to find best consulting minds where the participants have to make an approximate estimate to the given case without coplete information. The solution will be a rough approximation or an educated guess for which no better information would be available. The challenge is to use best possible approach to get closer to a reasonable solution. The whole purpose is to evaluate logical, analytical and interpretational skills.

Prizes

Win total cash prizes of ₹7000.
Total prize pool worth ₹15000.

Rules

  1. A team event, consisting teams of maximum 2 members. The team must register to participate in the event.
  2. A 3-round event in which the 1st round will be marked on the basis of standard deviation of the mid-point of the approximate ranges given as answers.
  3. The next 2 rounds would require the teams to walk through their approach to the problem to determine the answer to the question asked.
  4. In Round-1,a simple set of questions ,about 5 will be asked and the teams would have to give a range of approximation as their answers. The same printed questionaire would be given to all teams and they will be marked on the closeness of their estimation. An excel sheet will be created wherein the score for each team will be calculated on the basis of standard deviation. Time limit for solving the questionnaire would be 15 minutes,3 min for each question.
  5. Top 5 or 6 teams would proceed to the next round,Round-2 wherein 2 questions would be put forward for them to answer. The questions will be presented through ppt. Their answers this time will be a numeric figure (not a range ) and also a the formulation of their estimation will be required,their theory to reach that conclusion. Time limit for the round would be 20 min,10 min for each question.
  6. A break would be given to the teams so as to get time to compile the results.
  7. Top 2-3 teams would play the final round . This round would consist of a single question and each team would be required to give a figure of their estimation,their formulation and also present their solution to reach the conclusion. Time limit would be of 20 minutes for the question. The opposing team could also object and question the team presenting the theory. The team presenting must have a rock solid argument and analysis of their approach.
