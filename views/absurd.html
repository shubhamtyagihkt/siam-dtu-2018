Description

"Abstract mathematics is all about structure, veracity and the underlying beauty."
Can you amalgamate mathematics, the music of reason with abstraction which is secluded from realism? Do you have what it takes to fabricate untrue yet logical details to prove the speculative scenarios? Are you crafty enough to prove something so unpredictable like 2+2=5? If your answer to all these questions is yes, then ABSURD THEORY is undoubtedly the event for you to swank your skills!
The game tests the creativity of the students along with mathematical laws which though being absurd and irrelevant with respect to the theory, is intriguing enough to convince others.

  3. Mail the entries to- shakunt.trehan0810@gmail.com
    SUBJECT : ABSURD THEORY, topic you've selected.

  6. Mention your Name, Contact Number and College in the report as well as the email.

  8. The top 8 teams will qualify for the final round.

  12. Use of internet for the final round is strictly prohibited.
  13. Cross questioning by other teams and judges will be allowed after the completion of the presentation.
  14. The decision of the judges will be final and binding.
    15. Good luck fellas. It's time to bend the truth!
