Rules/Instructions
  1. You need to sign in first and then Cick Enter Quiz button to start the quiz.
  2. One question will be posted everyday at 7pm on the website till March 25.
  3. POINTS AND wik CREDITS will be added in the accounts of the participants who answer correctly.
  4. Be right and swift. The FIRST TWENTY CORRECT responses, get points on a rank based system. That is, the FIRST person to answer bags 30 points, SECOND: 29 points, THIRD: 28 points ... TWENTIETH: 11 points.
  5. All subsequent correct responses, gets 10 POINTS each.
  6. NO NEGATIVE MARKING. 0 points, for an incorrect response.
  7. Only one response will be recorded per participant per question.
  8. The participants can answer a question until the next question is posted. That is, you get 24 hours to solve a question.
  9. The result of the question of the day will be declared after the next question is posted.
Prizes
Daily
The TOP TWENTY participants of the day will earn Mobikwik credits on a rank based system as mentioned above which can be directly redeemed against the mobikwik add up vouchers.
Weekly
The TOP TWO participants with the maximum points for the ongoing week gets Mobikwik credits worth Rs.100 which can be directly redeemed against the mobikwik add up vouchers.
Contest Winner
The TOP scorer of the month gets Mobikwik credits worth Rs.500 which can be directly redeemed against the mobikwik add up vouchers.
Redemption method
  1. Your credits can directly be redeemed against the MobiKwik wallet addup vouchers.
  2. To redeem your credits just click on the Redeem button.
  3. Your credits will be redeemed to the nearest lower multiple of 20. Example: If you have have earned 70 credits and you decide to redeem your credits, 60 credits will be deducted from your credits and will be converted in vouchers. The 10 credits left will be there in your account only and you can grow them by answering more questions.
  4. You will recieve your voucher codes in 24 hrs after the redemption, through an email to your registered email id.
