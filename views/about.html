About SIAM
SIAM is a leading international organisation comprised of professionals and students whose primary interest is in mathematics and computational science and their applications.
The community builds a learning environment for its member students and elite professors' panel.It aims to provide a common platform to the students to allow them to freely discuss,brainstorm and work on various aspects of mathematics . SIAM DTU looks forward to involve students in the development of their mathematical prowess and widen their horizons to facilitate analytical thinking and enhance their reasoning powers.
