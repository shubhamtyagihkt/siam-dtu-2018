Description

Data Science is the most upcoming arena which holds infinite potential for growth and opportunities. Machine learning and Artificial Intelligence are the two most prominent fields of science that have seen immense research work in the past two decades.
We present to you our Annual Symposium on Data Science with our panel of wonderful achievers in these fields.

Speakers

DR. CHETAN ARORA

An Assistant Professor at IIIT-D, Dr. Chetan Arora has done his B.Tech and Ph.D from IIT Delhi. He has been involved in co-founding technology startups with his collaborators in India and Japan.
Before joining IIITD, he had been working as a post doctoral researcher at Hebrew University.
Dr. Arora works in the area of Computer Vision. His specific interests lie in Egocentric Vision and Combinatorial optimization algorithms specially for MRF-MAP inference.

DR. NILADRI CHATTERJEE

A Maths professor at IIT Delhi, Dr. Niladri Chatterjee is a veteran in the fields of Machine Translation and Artificial Intelligence. With a Ph.D. from University of London, Dr. Chatterjee also specializes in the fields of Reasoning, Statistical Modeling and Semantic Web.

DR. ANIRBAN MONDAL

Dr. Anirban Mondal, Associate Professor, SNU, will be one of the guest lecturers at SIAM DTU.
With a B.Tech in CSE from IIT-KGP, an MBA from University of Massachussets Amherst and a Ph.D. in Computer Science from the University of Singapore, Dr. Mondal is an expert in the arena of Big Data and Internet of Things.
His other fields of research are Large scale data management in distributed systems and for financial services.

MR. CHIRAG AHUJA

Mr. Chirag Ahuja, a data scientist and AI practitioner at Tatras Data is one of the guest lecturers who would be enlightening the students about Data Science at the Annual Symposium of SIAM DTU. His area of research is Deep Learning algorithms for Computer Vision, NLP and self driving cars.
With immense experience in this field as a previously Hadoop Consultant, Mr. Ahuja is bound have to have lots of information to share with the students.

MR. ANKUSH SINGLA

He needs no introduction, for every engineer is bound to know who he is. The cofounder at Coding Ninjas, Mr. Singla is a B.Tech from IIT-D and an MS from Stanford University.
He is a code enthusiast with previous experience at bigwigs like Amazon and Facebook.
