Description

Alumnus-Students interation session is an event aiming at the cherishment of old relationships, their celebration and refreshment, and also formation of new ones. It is a time of reflecting, and remembering the good old times and memories of the Alumnus, looking forward with a sense of purpose and anticipation.
SIAM-DTU plans to organise Alumnus-Students interation session to enlighten the students of Mathematics and Computing Branch, which would be the foundation of enhanced communication between the students and the alumnus, with the alumus playing the role of conscientious mentors to shape the careers of the future of our country. Through this interaction between students and alumnus, contacts are sure to be built, which could prove to be helpful for lifetime.
